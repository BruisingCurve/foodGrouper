Learn a bit more about FoodGroupr!

Here at foogroupr the mission is to help you find food easier. Connecting you to the great food found in neighborhoods all around you. You're busy so the goal is to give you the best set of options while limiting information overload. Maybe you're traveling with friends who have different food preferences to accomodate, maybe you're new to town and want to just explore what's around you, or maybe you're interested in finding something to eat without pinning your hopes on one restaurant that could be closed, jammed packed, or disappointing in person. All of these are great reasons to try foodgroupr.

Foodgroupr was developed from personal frustrations related to moveing and traveling, struggling to figure out where to head for the evening in each new town. Made as part of an Insightdatascience project, foodgroupr pulls local restaurant data from Foursquare through their generous API. Please hit up their site as well if you liked the tool.
